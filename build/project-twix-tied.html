TWIX — Tied To You. Twice.

2026 | Director of Photography

A narrative-led brand film inspired by the idea of invisible connections. Set within a quiet library, the film follows a character who discovers a red thread and begins to follow it across shifting spaces. Through a series of match cuts and continuous motion, the thread guides him through fragmented moments — blurring the line between reality and imagination.

The journey resolves as a dream, grounding back into a simple, personal moment: opening a Twix and eating both bars. Inspired by the "red string theory," the film reframes Twix as a connection — not between people, but between moments, choices, and repetition.
