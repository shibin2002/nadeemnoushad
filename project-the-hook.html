The Hook

2026 | Director of Photography

A behind-the-scenes look at The Hook, capturing the energy, preparation and personality behind their live performance. This film focuses on honest, unscripted moments from soundcheck to stage revealing the dynamic between the band and the atmosphere surrounding the event. The aim was to document the experience as it naturally unfolded, keeping the visuals intimate, raw and authentic.
